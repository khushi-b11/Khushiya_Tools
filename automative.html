Automotive Components & Assemblies

We have supplied a range of precision-manufactured components for the automotive sector, catering to both OEM vendors and local suppliers. Our facility is equipped to handle small-batch, highly customized mechanical parts that meet stringent tolerance and durability requirements.

Key Deliverables:

Scooter Gear Assemblies

Manufactured gear train components for two-wheelers, ensuring smooth meshing and long wear life under constant motion and friction.

Main & Side Stands

Built durable and weight-balanced stands for scooters and motorcycles using high-grade steel and robotic welding techniques.

Engine Mounting Brackets

Fabricated high-strength brackets used to mount engines securely onto vehicle frames, designed to resist heat and vibration.

Housing & Covers

Produced enclosures and metal casings for various under-hood components with corrosion-resistant finishes.

Custom Shafts & Pins

Delivered custom-sized shafts, pivots, and dowel pins for suspension and frame assemblies.
